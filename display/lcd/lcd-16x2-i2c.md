# I2C LCD 16x2

## Components
1. I2C LCD 16x2
2. ESP32-WROOM-32UE
3. Jumper Wires

## Connection(s)

| I2C LCD | ESP32 | Wire Color |
|:-------:|:--------:|:-------:|
| SDA | GPIO 21 (I2C SDA) | Orange |
| SCL | GPIO 22 (I2C SCL) | Yellow |
| VCC | 5V | Red |
| GND | GND | Black |
