# MPU-6050

## Components
1. ESP32-WROOM-32UE
2. MPU-6050
3. Female to Female Jumper Wires

## Connection(s)
| MPU | ESP32  |  Wire  |
|:---:|:------:|:------:|
| VCC | 3.3V   | Red    |
| GND | GND    | Black  |
| SDA | GPIO21 | Orange |
| SCL | GPIO22 | Yellow |
